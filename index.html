NuFlo Water Sensor Network

MVP - Real-time data tracking for rural water wells in Peru


At NuFlo, our mission is to provide real-time water quality data in remote regions to give organisations the necessary data to act.

Here is a model of our data interface. This shows different wells and the real-time data received, such as pH, lead levels, coliform, temperature, and TDS(Total Dissolved Solids).

Navigate the map below, clicking on safe wells (blue circles), unsafe wells (flashing), and our data gateways (red circles). Wells are connected to data gateways via green dashed lines.

Please note that this interface and well locations are not representative of real-world locations or conditions.
